About Me

Hi! I'm Maciej, a Level Designer from Wrocław, Poland. I studied Game Design at University of Lower Silesia, where I earned my Bachelor's degree. With 6 years of experience in level design, I continue to draw inspiration from any turn based games like chess and card games, but also rts, moba, fps, puzzle tps, rouge-likes and that shaped my passion for gaming. I see games as an interactive art form inspiring to find both rewarding and unpredictable.

I'm always ready for a new challenge!
